Voxelith is a browser-native voxel editor inspired by MagicaVoxel. Place, paint and carve cubes on an infinite grid with mirror symmetry, full undo history and a fast greedy-meshed renderer — then export to OBJ or glTF.

A voxel studio in the browser

Build, paint and carve — instantly.

Everything you need to model in voxels, with a clean interface and no download. Point, click, and watch your model take shape.

Place, erase, paint & pick

Four core tools with keyboard shortcuts. Build up geometry, carve it back, repaint faces and eyedrop colors — all directly on the 3D model.

🪞

Mirror symmetry

Model half, get the whole — X and Z axis symmetry for perfect builds.

↩️

Full undo history

Unlimited undo/redo so you can experiment fearlessly.

🎨

Rich color palette

A curated palette plus a custom color picker for any hue you need.

Export to OBJ & glTF

Take your creation anywhere. Voxelith writes clean, greedy-meshed geometry ready for Blender, game engines and 3D printing pipelines.

🧭

Orbit, pan & zoom

Fluid camera controls to inspect your model from any angle.
